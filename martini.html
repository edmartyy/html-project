INGREDIENTS

  • 50ml Christmas vodka*
  • 12.5ml Amaretto
  • 1 tsp Gingerbread syrup
  • 2 dashes of orange bitters

*Christmas Vodka (makes 1 bottle):

  • Zest of half an orange
  • 4 cloves
  • 1/3 cinnamon stick
  • 1 slice of fresh ginger
  • Half a grated nutmeg
  • 15ml maple syrup

Garnish:

  • Flames orange zest
  • Candy cane

Glass:

  • Martini glass

METHOD

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass
  2. Stir with ice
  3. Pour from a low height into a frozen martini glass
  4. Flame an orange zest over the drink then discard
  5. Hang candy cane on the rim of the glass
