INGREDIENTS

  • 35ml Bacardi Oakheart rum
  • 15ml Kahlua coffee liqueur
  • 15ml gingerbread syrup
  • 50ml espresso
  • 25ml cream
  • Dusting of chocolate powder

METHOD

  1. Add ingredients to a cocktail mixer and shake with ice to mix
  2. Pour through a strainer into a coupe glass
  3. Garnish with a dusting of chocolate powder and serve
